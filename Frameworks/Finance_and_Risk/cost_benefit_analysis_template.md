## Before You Start — Critical Instructions

> **The single most important rule of Cost-Benefit Analysis:**
> *"Never compare 'Apples to Oranges'. You must translate all costs and all benefits into a single unit of measurement: Money. Even 'intangible' benefits like 'employee morale' must be quantified (e.g., estimating the monetary savings of reduced staff turnover). If you cannot quantify it, you cannot accurately weigh it."*
> *(Source: CFI — Financial Modeling, 2025; Business-in-a-box — CBA Best Practices)*

**How to use this template:**

**Step 1** — Define the project and the specific decision you are trying to make.
**Step 2** — List all **Costs** (Direct, Indirect, Intangible, and Opportunity costs).
**Step 3** — List all **Benefits** (Revenue, Savings, and quantified Intangibles).
**Step 4** — Run the **Financial Metrics** (ROI, Payback Period, Net Benefit).
**Step 5** — Perform a **Sensitivity Check** (What happens if we are wrong?).

> ⚠️ **Research warning:** Do not use accounting *profit* to calculate your ROI. Use actual *cash flow* (cash in vs. cash out). Furthermore, always account for the **Opportunity Cost**: What else could we do with this money/time if we don't do this project?
> *(Source: Smartsheet — CBA Frameworks, 2025)*

> 🔁 **Review cycle:** Re-evaluate your CBA quarterly during the project execution to ensure the costs are not ballooning past the expected benefits.

## When to Use This Framework

| Situation | How This Template Helps |
|---|---|
| Capital Expenditures (CapEx) | Proves to the CFO that buying a $100k machine will actually save $150k in labor over 3 years |
| Hiring Decisions | Determines if hiring a new $80k employee will generate more than $80k in value |
| Policy Changes | Helps HR prove that a new $10k wellness program will save $30k in health premiums and turnover |

*(Source: Atlassian — Project Justification, 2025)*

---

## Framework Limitations — Know What It Cannot Do

| Limitation | What to Use Instead |
|---|---|
| Deeply Complex, Multi-Year Finance | If the project spans 5+ years and involves inflation, debt financing, and depreciation, use a formal **DCF (Discounted Cash Flow) / NPV Model** built by an accountant. |
| Ethical or Safety Decisions | You cannot put a price tag on human life or core ethical values. If a project is required for safety or legal compliance, a CBA is irrelevant. |
